How it started


As first years that struggled to find a common platform where we could compare all the different University of Manchester accommodations based on reviews, pictures, and facts, we decided to make the lives of future students easier. Our website, MARSS is specifically designed for University of Manchester students with all the ratings and comments reliably provided by current students at the university. Information about each hall of residence can be found along with a map, comments, and ratings based on specific criteria. Find your Student Space now.
